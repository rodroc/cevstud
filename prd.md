## Direction: keep it essentially black and white and let the lime be the one accent. Use it for CTAs and headlines, and bring in tasteful lime gradients to feel modern. Use the dark olive as a supporting tone where flat lime would be too loud. Show judgment on where lime adds energy vs. where restraint keeps it classy.
